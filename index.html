About Me

I'm Addor Daniel Akankoba — a self-taught front-end developer who builds clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I believe in learning through doing, and I treat every project like it's mine.

I started coding from scratch using free resources and real-world projects, pushing myself to grow daily. Whether it's writing clean JavaScript, styling with Bootstrap, or building smart UIs with React — I'm all in.

When I’m not coding, I’m learning, tweaking, or thinking about how to improve what I’ve already built. This site is both my portfolio and my proof.

A collection of small but meaningful projects created as part of my journey into frontend development.
